Survey & Quote

Rotherham covers a wide area of South Yorkshire, from the town centre and Kimberworth in the west through to Wickersley, Maltby and Dinnington in the east, and from Rawmarsh and Wath-upon-Dearne in the north down to Brinsworth and Treeton in the south. The housing stock is enormously varied — Victorian and Edwardian brick terraces in the older parts of town, 1920s and 1930s semis across the interwar suburbs, post-war council estates, and more recent private developments.

For the older terraced properties — particularly the solid-wall Victorian houses that make up much of Rotherham's inner streets — external wall insulation makes an immediate difference. These homes were built without cavity walls and lose a significant proportion of their heat directly through the external brickwork. An EWI system adds insulation on the outside, finished with a silicone or acrylic render topcoat. The result is a warmer home, meaningfully lower energy bills, and a transformation in how the property looks from the street.

Across the interwar and post-war semis that dominate areas like Wickersley, Rawmarsh and Maltby, silicone render is the most popular choice. It is flexible enough to accommodate minor movement in older brickwork without cracking, hydrophobic so it stays clean in wet conditions, and available in any colour so the finish can be completely tailored to the property. We survey before we quote — assessing the condition of the substrate, identifying any defects that need addressing first, and recommending the right system for the building and the budget.

02 Rendering
What render system suits older terraced and semi-detached properties in Rotherham?
+

Silicone render is the most popular choice across Rotherham's pre-war and post-war housing stock. It bonds firmly to brick and blockwork, creates a clean weatherproof finish, and its hydrophobic surface means rain runs off rather than soaking in. For Rotherham's older terraced streets or 1950s–1970s semis, silicone render is transformative — it covers tired brickwork, stops moisture ingress and dramatically improves the appearance of the property. Monocouche is an alternative for homeowners who prefer a more textured, rustic look with a natural through-colour finish.

03 Insulation
Can you fit external wall insulation on a solid-wall Victorian terrace in Rotherham?
+

Yes — EWI is ideal for Rotherham's Victorian and Edwardian solid-wall terraces, which lose a substantial amount of heat through their external walls. The system involves fixing an insulation board to the exterior, then finishing with a silicone or acrylic render topcoat. The result is a significantly warmer home with lower energy bills and a completely fresh external appearance. We carry out a free survey first to assess the wall construction, confirm suitability and specify the correct insulation thickness for the property.

04 Timescales
How long does it take to render a semi-detached in Rotherham?
+

A standard semi-detached property in Rotherham typically takes 3 to 5 days to render from start to finish. The programme covers preparation — cleaning, masking, fixing any defective substrate — followed by the basecoat system and the final silicone or monocouche topcoat. Timescales vary slightly depending on the size of the property and the weather. We cannot apply render in frost or heavy rain, so we plan around the forecast. A clear schedule is agreed at quotation stage so you know exactly when work will start and finish.

05 Full Service
Do you carry out internal plastering in Rotherham as well as external rendering?
+

Yes — we offer a full interior and exterior plastering and rendering service across Rotherham. As well as external render systems, we carry out internal plastering including full re-plasters, skim coats over plasterboard, patch repairs after building work or damp treatment, and dry lining. Many homeowners in Rotherham use us for both internal and external work at the same time — it saves disruption, reduces set-up costs, and means one contractor is responsible for the entire job. Let us know what you need at the survey stage and we will quote for everything together.
