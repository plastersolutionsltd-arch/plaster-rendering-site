What render is best for a Victorian property in Nether Edge?
+

For Nether Edge's Victorian and Edwardian properties, monocouche scratch render is often the best choice — it's a through-colour textured finish that suits the period character. Silicone render works well on brick or previously rendered surfaces where a smoother finish is preferred.

Can you do EWI on a large Victorian semi in Nether Edge?
+

Yes — we regularly work on larger period properties in S7. We assess party walls, window reveals and roof details carefully, and ensure the system is fully detailed. 90mm boards, choice of silicone or monocouche finish, 25-year warranty.
