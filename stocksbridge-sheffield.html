Questions

Stocksbridge FAQ

01 Coverage
Do you cover Stocksbridge S36?
+

Yes, we cover all of S36 including Stocksbridge, Deepcar, Bolsterstone and the surrounding Upper Don Valley. We're based in Grenoside about 15 minutes away. Free surveys, no call-out charge.

02 Rendering
What render is most popular in Stocksbridge?
+

Silicone render is the most popular choice in Stocksbridge — it's weatherproof, flexible and comes with a 15-year colour guarantee, which matters in an exposed location like the Upper Don Valley. Monocouche is popular on older Victorian properties for a more traditional finish.

03 Insulation
Is EWI worth it in Stocksbridge given the cold winters?
+

Absolutely — Stocksbridge is one of the colder parts of Sheffield due to its altitude and exposed position. EWI adds 90mm of insulation to the outer wall, dramatically reducing heat loss. Most customers see a noticeable reduction in heating bills within the first winter.

04 Timescales
How long does rendering take on a Stocksbridge terrace?
+

A typical Stocksbridge mid-terrace takes 2-3 days for silicone render. A semi-detached is 3-5 days. We include all preparation, priming, mesh and topcoat. You get a fixed price before we start.

05 Full Service
Do you do plastering inside as well as rendering outside in Stocksbridge?
+

Yes, full internal service — re-skims, new plasterboard, stud partitions and ceilings. Many Stocksbridge customers combine external render with internal plastering on renovation projects.
