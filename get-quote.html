Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our quoting process and services.

How long does it take to get a quote?

We aim to provide quotes within 24 business hours of receiving your request. For complex projects, we may arrange a site survey first to ensure accuracy. A typical survey takes 30–60 minutes.

Is the quote really free with no obligation?

Absolutely. Our quotes are completely free and come with zero obligation. We believe in transparent, honest pricing with no hidden costs or pressure to commit.

What information do you need for an accurate quote?

For a detailed quote, we typically need your address, property type, description of the work needed, and your timeline. For complex projects, an on-site survey helps us provide the most accurate estimate.

Can you provide a quote over the phone?

We can give you a rough estimate over the phone, but for accurate pricing, we prefer to assess the site properly. This ensures you get a true quote and helps us avoid any surprises during the work.

Do you require a deposit before starting work?

No deposit required. You only pay when the work is completed and you're satisfied with the results. This is our no-deposit security policy.

What's included in your guarantee?

All our work comes with a 5-year workmanship guarantee. Materials (silicone render, monocouche, etc.) are backed by manufacturer guarantees of up to 25 years. You're completely covered.

Can you work around my schedule?

Absolutely. We're flexible with timing and can discuss scheduling once your quote is approved. Whether you need work done urgently or prefer to plan ahead, we'll accommodate your needs.
