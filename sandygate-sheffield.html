02 Rendering
What render suits Sandygate's large Victorian properties?
+

For larger Victorian and Edwardian properties in Sandygate, monocouche scratch render is often the best choice — it gives a traditional textured finish that suits the property's age and style, while providing excellent weather protection. Silicone render is also popular for a smoother, more contemporary look. We'll advise at the free survey stage on which system is most appropriate for your specific property.

03 Colour Matching
Can you match existing render on a Sandygate property?
+

Yes. Colour matching is something we take seriously. We use premium render systems from K Rend, Weber and Ecorend with extensive colour ranges. For properties where part of the render is being replaced or repaired, we take the time to get the match right. In some cases where an exact match isn't achievable, we'll advise on rendering the whole elevation for a consistent result.

04 Internal
Do you handle large internal plastering projects in Sandygate?
+

Yes, we take on larger internal plastering projects throughout Sandygate. Whether it's a full house re-skim as part of a renovation, ornate plasterwork repairs on period features, or new partition walls on an extension project, we have the experience to handle complex work on larger properties. We provide a full written quote before any work starts.

05 Insulation
Is EWI suitable for older Sandygate properties?
+

EWI can work very well on larger properties, though we always survey first — some period properties in Sandygate are better suited to internal insulation to preserve the external character and detailing. We'll advise honestly on what's right for your property rather than simply pushing one solution. Where EWI is appropriate, the transformation in both appearance and energy performance is substantial.
