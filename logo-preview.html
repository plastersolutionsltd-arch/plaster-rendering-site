06 — Design Notes
Tweaks & recommendations
Concept A — Recommended
Closest to your current logo but with a fully solid R (not outline). The overlap creates a strong monogram that reads as one mark. Best for business cards and website.
Concept B — Best for Embroidery
No overlap means cleaner stitching at small sizes. The P+R block reads well on workwear from a distance. Simpler to digitise — fewer thread colour transitions.
What I changed from your original
R is now fully solid (not outline). Changed "PLASTER RENDER SPECIALISTS" to use centre dots for better visual flow. Green updated to #37D413 (your website green). Added a horizontal lockup for van signage.
For print (business cards)
Tell your printer: Green = Pantone 375 C. Ask for silk laminate finish on dark cards — it makes them feel premium. Minimum 400gsm card weight recommended.
