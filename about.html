Our Story

A local trade built on doing it properly

Plaster and Rendering Solutions was founded in 2012 with one simple aim: to give Sheffield homeowners plastering and rendering they can rely on — done by qualified people, finished to a standard we'd accept in our own homes.

The business was started by Chris Glover, who has spent over 25 years working in the plastering and rendering trade. What began as one tradesman with a van and a reputation for tidy, honest work has grown into one of Sheffield's most trusted rendering teams — with more than 100 five-star Google reviews to show for it.

We're deliberately not the biggest firm in the city. Every job is overseen personally by Chris, so the person quoting your work is the person responsible for it. Nothing gets handed off to a stranger, and nothing gets rushed. From a single skimmed ceiling to a full external wall insulation system, the same standard applies.

We work across every Sheffield postcode, S1 to S36, as well as Rotherham, Barnsley and the wider South Yorkshire area — and because we're approved applicators for leading render manufacturers, we can back our external work with manufacturer warranties of up to 25 years.

Founder & Lead Plasterer

Chris Glover

Chris is an NVQ-qualified plasterer with more than 25 years' hands-on experience across domestic and commercial projects in Sheffield. He's spent his career mastering everything from traditional float-and-skim plastering to modern silicone render and external wall insulation systems. He personally surveys, prices and oversees every job the company takes on — and he's usually the one you'll meet at your front door.

Common Questions

Good to know

Who will actually carry out my work?
+

Your work is carried out by Chris Glover and his small, trusted team — never subcontracted out to strangers. Chris is NVQ-qualified with 25+ years' experience and personally oversees every job from the first survey to final handover.

How long have you been trading?
+

Plaster and Rendering Solutions Limited was founded in 2012 and has served Sheffield ever since. Founder Chris Glover has over 25 years of hands-on experience in the trade.

Are you qualified and accredited?
+

Yes. Chris holds a full NVQ in Plastering. The company is CHAS accredited for health & safety, and is an approved applicator for K-Rend and Ecorend render systems — letting us issue manufacturer warranties of up to 25 years.

What areas do you cover?
+

Every Sheffield postcode from S1 to S36 — including Dore, Fulwood, Crosspool, Hillsborough, Crookes, Walkley, Chapeltown, Grenoside, Nether Edge and Woodseats — plus Rotherham, Barnsley and the wider South Yorkshire area.

Do you ask for a deposit?

No deposit required. You only pay once the work is finished and you're completely satisfied. Every job is backed by a 5-year workmanship guarantee.
