01 Cost
How much does rendering cost in Sheffield?

Rendering a house in Sheffield costs £65–£90 per m² installed, with materials and scaffolding included. As a guide: acrylic from around £65/m², monocouche £70/m², silicone-enhanced £75/m² and premium silicone £85/m². A typical 3-bed semi works out at roughly £5,500–£9,900. Every quote is free with no deposit required.

+
02 Choosing
What is the best render for a Sheffield home?

For most Sheffield homes monocouche offers the best value — a through-coloured single-coat render that never needs painting. Silicone render is the premium choice: flexible, self-cleaning and crack-resistant with a 20–25 year lifespan and 100+ colours. Sand and cement suits conservation areas and matching existing render. We advise honestly at the free survey.

+
03 Timescale
How long does rendering a house take?

A typical semi-detached house takes around two weeks including scaffolding, preparation and finishing — our Millhouses external wall insulation case study went up, rendered and finished in a fortnight. Small repairs and single walls take a few days. Weather affects external work, so we schedule around suitable conditions.

+
04 Planning
Do I need planning permission to render my house?

In most cases, rendering a standard residential property falls under Permitted Development and does not require planning permission. Exceptions include listed buildings, conservation area properties, apartment blocks, and some flats. We advise on planning requirements at survey.

+
05 Repairs
Can cracked render be repaired without re-rendering the whole house?

Usually, yes. Where most of the render is sound, we cut out and patch the failed sections, colour-matched to the existing finish — targeted repairs typically start from around £800. If failure is widespread, a full re-render is often better value. The free survey tells you honestly which you need.

+
06 Warranty
What warranty comes with rendering work?

We are approved applicators for K-Rend, Ecorend and Weber, so silicone and monocouche systems carry manufacturer warranties of up to 25 years. On top of that, every job comes with our five-year workmanship guarantee. Warranties are only valid when systems are installed by approved applicators — which is exactly what we are.

+
07 Coverage
What areas of Sheffield do you cover for rendering?

We cover every Sheffield postcode from S1 to S36 — including Hillsborough, Chapeltown, Ecclesfield, Dore, Fulwood, Crosspool, Walkley, Woodseats and Totley — plus Rotherham and wider South Yorkshire. We travel from our base in Grenoside with no call-out charge, and every survey is free.
